# Dashboard Wireframe

## Page 1: Executive Overview

Top row:

- Total Income
- Total Expenses
- Net Savings
- Savings Rate
- Average Monthly Expense

Middle row:

- Monthly Income vs Expenses, clustered column chart
- Category-wise Expense Breakdown, donut or horizontal bar chart

Bottom row:

- Data Story insight cards for savings pressure, controllable spend, recurring baseline, and cash reliance
- Spending Trend, line chart
- Recurring Payments, table

Global slicers:

- Month
- Category
- Transaction Type

## Page 2: Cash and Recurring Behavior

- Cash Withdrawal Trend by Month
- Recurring Expense by Merchant
- Recurring Payment Detail Table
- Mode-wise Spending

## Page 3: Transaction Explorer

- Transaction table with columns: date, merchant, description, mode, category, deposit, withdrawal, balance
- Search/filter pane for merchant, category, mode, month
- Conditional formatting on debit and credit amounts
